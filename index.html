    About Us
    The Housing & Development Board (HDB) is Singapore's public housing authority and a statutory board under the Ministry of National Development. We develop public housing to provide Singaporeans with affordable, quality homes, and a better living environment. Focusing on nurturing a strong family and community spirit, public housing policies and schemes are formulated to meet changing needs and aspirations.

    Phased Resumption of HDB’s Services and Construction Activities after Circuit Breaker Period

    29 May 2020

    The Government has announced its decision to exit the Circuit Breaker and resume economic and business activities in phases from 2 June 2020.

    CPF INTEREST RATES FROM 1 JULY 2020 TO 30 SEPTEMBER 2020

    22 May 2020

    CPF members below 55 years old will be paid an extra 1% interest on the first $60,000 of their combined balances (with up to $20,000 from the OA).

    HDB Launches Residential Site at Yishun Avenue 9

    30 Apr 2020

    The Housing & Development Board (HDB), as the Government’s land sales agent has launched a residential site at Yishun Avenue 9 for sale by public tender today, under the Confirmed List of 1st Half 2020 Government Land Sales (GLS) Programme.
